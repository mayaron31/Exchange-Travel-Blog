A Diary of a Six-Month Journey in Europe

Zurich

I began my journey with a two-day trip to Switzerland with my parents. Beautiful scenery and friendly people, but very expensive!

Strasbourg

I continued with a night in Strasbourg. A charming city rich in history where I had one of the best burgers ever!

Black Forest

I visited the Black Forest multiple times, including Titisee Lake, Freiburg, and Triberg Falls. Highlights included Europa Park and popular alpine sledding!

Oktoberfest

Spent two nights with exchange friends in Munich at Oktoberfest. It was hot and crowded but unforgettable—I drank more beer than ever before!

Italy

During the holidays, I went couch surfing in Milan, explored Tuscany, Bologna, and Modena, and stayed with my cousin in Rome. The food was incredible!

Prague

A spontaneous trip to a city I always wanted to visit. I explored Prague on foot for three days—cheap, fun, and stunning, especially in autumn!

London

Returned to my favorite city and reunited with my sister. We enjoyed Winter Wonderland, Christmas markets, and a Les Misérables musical!

Berlin

A spontaneous trip with two exchange friends to Berlin. Cheap train rides made it easy. We partied, ate Israeli food, and felt the Christmas spirit.

Christmas Markets in Germany

I visited several Christmas markets across Germany—Cologne (the most beautiful), Heidelberg, Frankfurt, Nuremberg, and Darmstadt. I drank lots of Glühwein!
